FerCal Alerts

SMS Notification Service

FerCal Alerts is a private, automated SMS notification service that delivers real-time sports betting signal alerts to registered users.

What messages will I receive?

  • Pre-game sharp money alerts when betting signals meet defined thresholds
  • In-game line movement notifications during live events
  • System status alerts (e.g. session or data feed issues)

Message frequency varies by day depending on game schedules. Typically 0–5 messages per day.

How to Opt In

This is a private service. Participation is by request only. To request access, email prestinario@gmail.com with the subject line "FerCal Alerts Opt-In" and include the mobile number you'd like to register.

By submitting a request you consent to receive automated SMS alerts from FerCal Alerts on the number provided. Standard message and data rates may apply.

How to Opt Out

Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe immediately. Reply HELP for support information.

Privacy

Phone numbers are used solely to deliver alerts and are never shared with third parties.
